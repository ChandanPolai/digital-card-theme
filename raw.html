WOODEN BOXES

At Patel Packaging Pvt. Ltd., we prioritize secure, durable and eco-friendly packaging. Our wooden boxes provide superior protection while enhancing aesthetics with their natural grain and smooth finish. Designed for both storage and shipping, they ensure your products arrive safely.

We specialize in crafting wooden boxes capable of handling up to 25 tons, offering unmatched strength for heavy-duty applications. With a commitment to sustainability and quality, our packaging solutions blend practicality with visual appeal, making them both functional and attractive for all your packaging needs.

WOODEN SADDLES PACKAGING

Supporting the giants of industry, Patel Packaging Pvt. Ltd. Excels in transportation saddles and Hydrotest saddles. Designed with precision, these saddles bear immense loads with unmatched strength. With years of trust from Larsen & Toubro (L&T), our expertise speaks for itself.

We specialize in creating customised wooden saddles, ensuring a secure foundation for your vessels. From design to delivery, we provide reliability you can count on every step of the way.

WOODEN SKID PACKAGING

Amidst the bustling demands of global trade, wooden skid packaging stands as the silent hero for heavy-duty transport. Acting as a sturdy platform, skids carry massive capital goods with ease, making them a favourite for overseas shipments. These cost-saving heavy-duty 25-ton wooden skids provide a solid foundation, ensuring heavy machinery remains mobile and securely lifted off the ground. When size and strength matter most, our wooden skids deliver, ensuring your goods travel safely no matter the distance.

ANTI-CORROSION VCI PACKAGING

Protecting your metals from corrosion has never been easier with VCI packaging from Patel Packaging Pvt. Ltd. Our Volatile Corrosion Inhibitor (VCI) film is infused with innovative VCI chemistry, forming an invisible barrier on the surface of metals. This protective layer prevents corrosion caused by moisture, oxygen and contaminants.

Available in various sizes, weights and inhibitor types, our VCI packaging comes in rolls, bags, covers, and customised & printed options. Choose Patel Packaging Pvt. Ltd. to ensure your metal products remain safe and secure throughout storage and transportation.

VACUUM PACKAGING

When it comes to safeguarding large, complex equipment, Patel Packaging Pvt. Ltd. delivers unmatched protection with extra-strong 4-layer aluminum foil. Using advanced vacuum sealing, we meticulously remove air from the aluminum barrier, creating a robust shield against moisture, corrosion, and external damage. Each step, from precise wrapping to airtight sealing, is executed with expertise. Our packaging ensures the longevity of your valuable equipment, even in the harshest environments.

HEAT SHRINK WRAPPING

Odd shapes, challenging dimensions, and unpredictable journeys - Heat Shrink Wrapping securing your job. With expertise in on-site and on-board vessel services, Patel Packaging Pvt. Ltd. ensures a precise, tight wrap that conforms to your product's unique shape.

This specialized technique seals equipment effectively, protecting it during transport while maintaining its integrity. Whether it's industrial equipment or irregularly shaped goods, our heat shrink solutions offer reliability and peace of mind, making your equipment ready to face any journey with confidence.

ON SITE LASHING AND STUFFING

We take full responsibility for the lashing and stuffing of finished goods onto trailer beds and containers. As a trusted partner of L&T-MHPS Boilers Pvt. Ltd., we successfully dispatch over 1 lakh MT. Our skilled team specializes in securing various job types, including machinery, automobiles, steel coils and project equipment. Whether it's containers, trailers, or flatracks, we ensure your goods are safely secured for transport. Additionally, we offer a full range of lashing, lifting and hoisting products to meet all your job needs.

CONTAINER LASHING AND STUFFING

At Patel Packaging Pvt. Ltd., we ensure your equipment's journey begins and ends safely with expert container lashing and stuffing. From securing containers with precision using locks to packing goods with care, every step is designed to prevent damage and ensure stability, even in rough seas. Adhering to strict international standards, we prioritise efficiency, safety and protection for your valuable goods.

With Patel Packaging Pvt. Ltd., your equipment isn't just packed; it's safeguarded every mile of the way.

SEAWORTHY PACKAGING

When it comes to seaworthy packaging, Patel Packaging Pvt. Ltd. ensures your products, like automotive parts, reach their destination safely and intact. We offer a complete range of export-orientated packaging solutions, including wooden boxes, crates, skids, pallets and saddles. Our dedicated team works tirelessly to provide stress-free turnkey export packaging services.

Whether it's for contract packaging or specialised needs, we deliver on time, ensuring goods remain secure, no matter the journey, with guaranteed quality and precision every time.

TARPAULINS COVER

At Patel Packaging Pvt. Ltd., we offer multi-purpose tarpaulins and customized covers designed to protect your valuable vessels in any weather. Whether it's rain or shine, our heavy-duty tarpaulins provide unmatched durability and protection, especially during the monsoon. With in-house industrial stitching and pneumatic sealing machines, we ensure precision and quality in every cover. Lightweight yet incredibly strong, our tarpaulins are water and UV resistant, making them ideal for all industries. Available in

standard sizes or tailored to your specifications, Patel Packaging Pvt. Ltd. ensures your vessels stay safe and secure, no matter the conditions.

WOODEN PALLETS

At Patel Packaging Pvt. Ltd., we provide high-quality wooden pallets for seamless logistics, offering durability and cost-effectiveness for transportation and storage. Our range includes heavy-duty stringer pallets and stable block pallets, all compliant with international shipping regulations. With a weight capacity of 1 ton to 3 ton, our wooden pallets undergo heat treatment (ISPM-15) to meet global export standards. Additionally, we have an in-house heat treatment plants to ensure quality and compliance. Customization options are available to perfectly suit your business needs and elevate your supply chain.
